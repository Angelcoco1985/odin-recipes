Chicken Soup

Description

This meal is a favorite amongst cold people and sick people. It is warm and has a very good taste. It is also healthy and uses whole natural ingridients.

ingredients

  • Chicken
  • chicken boullon
  • carrots
  • pasta shells
  • cilantro

Steps

  1. Boil and shred chicken
  2. set water to boil and add pasta
  3. Add carrots and chicken boullon
  4. when almost ready add chicken back as well as the cilantro
  5. Enjoy!
