Ribeye Steak

Description

This meal is very high in protein and low ingridient. Red meat is one of the most nutrient rich food sources in the world. If made well the meat can taste very good.

Ingredients

  • Ribeye steak
  • Grassfed Butter
  • Rosemary

Steps

  1. Set pan to medium high heat
  2. let steak cook each side for 3 minutes
  3. Add butter and rosemary halfway through cook
  4. Pull out steak and lest rest for 5 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
