Tuna sandwich

Description

This meal is easy to make and very cost effective. The tuna is mixed with japanese and mayo. The bread is toasted with butter. It is delicious.

Ingredients

  • Sourdough bread
  • Fresh canned wild caught tuna
  • butter
  • japanese mayo
  • lettuce
  • corn

Steps

  1. Open can of tuna and corn and drain
  2. Mix tuna, corn, and mayo into a bowl
  3. Butter bread and cook on stove with low heat
  4. Once bread is golden brown take off and put tuna inbetween both slices
  5. Enjoy!
